FAQ

Do you take insurance?

No — AZ SLP is private pay only. A superbill can be provided upon request for possible out-of-network reimbursement (depends on your plan).

Do you only do feeding therapy?

Yes — the practice is focused exclusively on feeding therapy for children and adults.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes. Home visits are available across the Phoenix metro. Teletherapy is also available depending on your needs.

How do we get started?

Book a free 10-minute consult. If it’s a good fit, we’ll schedule an evaluation and create a clear home plan.
